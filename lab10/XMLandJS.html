What is XML?

  • XML was designed to store and transport data.
  • The XML language has no predefined tags.
  • "eXtensible markup language" stands for XML.
  • It lets you define your own tags and seperates information from presentation.

How is XML used?

The way XML is used is by creating readable tags for humans to explain the information presented on the page or website.
